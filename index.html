    Final-Statement

    The final version of Packet has a solution for every pain point I originally intended to solve. Users can install Packet, set up their own personal Packet profile, and start connecting in a few short minutes. Say goodbye to missed connections.

    Packet is the nanswer to the stagnated social mindset of the modern user. It is here to assist anti-social users with making friends in the real world, and the social users with a thoughtlessly easy way to connect on the fly. It can be used for organized meed ups, professiona networking, dating, or just meeting new people.
